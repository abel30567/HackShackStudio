Schedule 2017

HackLabs

HackLabs are for kids who are crazy about technology, and want to develop serious makers skills. They require a longer yet flexible commitment, so that they can master skills and complete projects. At the end of a HackLabs series, kids participate in the HackShowCase, where they show their completed projects to their family and friends.

Call us 3057477522 or write us to reserve your spot today.

HackShops

HackShops are for kids who are creative and curious, and want to explore the makers world. HackShops are designed to be short and immersive, and expose kids to particular tools and skills based on what they are interested in. HackShops are great for beginners and kids with a busy schedule.

HackCamps

When school's out, come to HackShack and make something awesome. We'll be Minecraft modding, hi and lo tech tinkering and playing with robots. Snacks included, bring your favorite lunch. We like big green salads. Use coupon code NEWBIECAMP for a special discount.

Hackathons

Hackathons are where tech-savvy kids show off their skills in a collaborative, but competitive environment. Cool rewards, fun badges, prizes and most importantly bragging rights are always on the line.

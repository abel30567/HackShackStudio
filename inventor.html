Inventor’s Workshop

At HackShack’s Inventor’s Workshop, we come together to make cool things using high-tech materials, such as LED Lights and recyclables. No prior experience is needed as everyone is an inventor at heart.

We make it easy, encouraging teamwork and providing prompts and guidance as our little ones design their invention.

What to expect

The Inventor’s workshop is held at HackShack Studio’s Makers’ space. It is limited to 8 participants (not including parents). Parents are welcome to join in the fun, or hang out in the parent’s lounge. Drop-off is also available. Parking is available on site at no charge.

The Inventor’s workshop lasts for 2 hours, from 10:30 AM to 12:30 PM. HackShack provides all of the materials, which include LED lights, cardboard, glue, paper products and glitter, among others. LittleBits circuits and motors can be used as well. They are available for purchase or bring your own. Every inventor takes their invention home.

The cost per participant is $25.00.
